>>> About:
‍
ArtNet.Live is a livestream event meant to promote new and inspiring musical talents. By giving artists a platform to network and collaborate, via our Discord server, we plan on shining a light to all the different disciplines and capabilities they own. Starting off as a way to showcase the talented people we met on the Metaverse, ArtNet.Live quickly became the platform we needed to prove that our voices, even if low, can still be heard facing the pressures of the industry. Built by people from all over the world, ArtNet.Live is a safe space where we can connect through shared interests and feel as, even if far away, we are closer than we think.

‍
‍The first volume of the event has been hosted March 5th, 2022.
Vol.II is set to be on June 30th 2022 at 20:00 (CET) & 2PM (EST).
